Compact status or category marker — for states, phases, and labels.

```jsx
<Badge variant="success" dot>Live</Badge>
<Badge variant="honey">In progress</Badge>
<Badge variant="ink">AI adoption</Badge>
```

Variants: `neutral` (default), `ink` (aubergine fill, pink type), `honey`, `clay`, `success`, `warning`, `danger`, `info`. Add `dot` for a leading status dot. Keep the label to one or two words; for longer, multi-word categories use `Tag` instead.
